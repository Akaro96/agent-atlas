# Comparison

## Positioning

This kit is intentionally smaller and more opinionated than the biggest public harnesses.

## Compared With Larger Public Harnesses

### `clarc`

Strengths:

- much larger command and agent surface
- broader workflow operating system feel
- strong showcase factor

Tradeoff:

- heavier to maintain
- harder to adapt cleanly to a single user's real machine

### `everything-claude-code`

Strengths:

- broad feature coverage
- lots of patterns and modules
- strong community-distribution posture

Tradeoff:

- larger cognitive surface
- easier to over-install things you do not need

## This Kit

Strengths:

- leaner
- dual-agent aware
- workspace and vault separation
- Windows-friendly
- validation-first
- portable helper tools included
- end-to-end smoke-testable

Tradeoff:

- less theatrical feature breadth
- fewer built-in commands than the biggest workflow operating systems

## Intended Audience

This kit fits people who want a serious daily-driver harness, not just an impressive screenshot of one.
